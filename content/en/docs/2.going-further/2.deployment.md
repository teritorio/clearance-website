# Deployment

Clearance is available in two deployment models to suit your needs and constraints.

## SaaS — Managed service

Teritorio offers Clearance as a **managed service**. We handle hosting, maintenance, and updates.

**Benefits:**

- No infrastructure to manage
- Automatic updates
- Technical support included
- Quick deployment

**Included:**

- Dedicated Clearance instance
- Rules configuration adapted to your scope
- Monitoring and maintenance
- Email support

With a monthly or annual SaaS subscription, **software maintenance** (corrective and evolutive) is included.

## Self-hosted

As Clearance is free software (AGPL-3.0 license), you can deploy it on your own infrastructure.

**Benefits:**

- Full control over your data
- Advanced customization
- No vendor dependency

**Prerequisites:**

- Linux server with Docker
- Internet connection for the OSM replication feed
- Storage space adapted to your geographic scope

## Business model

We offer Clearance hosting as a SaaS service as well as deployment and configuration assistance on your own servers. In both cases, guidance adapted to your project is included.

For each project, the following items should be considered:

- **Configuration and deployment** — Project configuration and deployment service, according to your quality constraints (tags, geometry, users, control rules)
- **Custom development** — Development of features specific to your needs
- **Free software contribution** — A contribution to the maintenance and evolution of the free software
