# References

Over 40 organizations already use Clearance to reuse OpenStreetMap data with greater control.

## SNCF Réseau

**SNCF Réseau** (French railways) uses Clearance to ensure the maintenance of track and signaling information in OpenStreetMap.

## Civil Protection of Navarre

The **Civil Protection of Navarre** (Spain) uses Clearance to guarantee the quality of road and addressing data used for dispatching emergency services. OSM data is used as the reference for calculating intervention routes.

## Departmental agencies and tourism offices

**30 departmental agencies and tourism and territorial attractiveness offices** (mainland and overseas France) control quality and monitor OSM contributions across many themes: leisure facilities, health, food shops, etc. These users hold a significant number of modifications for inspection.

[See an example: project in Landes](https://clearance.teritorio.xyz/france_landes_poi/changes_logs){target="_blank"}

## Why they use Clearance

Organizations that use Clearance share a common need: **depending on OpenStreetMap data while controlling its quality**.

- They cannot afford erroneous edits affecting their services
- They don't have the resources to manually monitor all changes
- They want to contribute to OpenStreetMap while maintaining quality control
