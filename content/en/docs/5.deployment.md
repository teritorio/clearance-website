# Deployment

Clearance is available in two deployment models to suit your needs and constraints.

## SaaS — Managed service

Teritorio offers Clearance as a **managed service**. We handle hosting, maintenance, and updates.

**Benefits:**

- No infrastructure to manage
- Automatic updates
- Technical support included
- Quick deployment

**Included:**

- Dedicated Clearance instance
- Rules configuration adapted to your scope
- Monitoring and maintenance
- Email support

## Self-hosted

As Clearance is open source (MIT license), you can deploy it on your own infrastructure.

**Benefits:**

- Full control over your data
- Advanced customization
- No vendor dependency

**Prerequisites:**

- Linux server with Docker
- Internet connection for the OSM replication feed
- Storage space adapted to your geographic scope

## Business model

Clearance is **free open source software**. Teritorio's business model relies on:

- **SaaS service** — Managed hosting and maintenance
- **Support** — Technical and functional guidance
- **Custom development** — Features specific to your needs
