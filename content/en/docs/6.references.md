# References

Clearance is used in production by organizations that depend on quality OpenStreetMap data for their services.

## Local authorities

French local authorities use Clearance to secure the map data that feeds their digital public services: interactive maps, route calculators, open data portals.

## Transport operators

Transport operators use Clearance to guarantee the quality of stop, line, and network data that feeds passenger information applications.

## Tourism offices

Tourism offices use Clearance to maintain reliable and up-to-date points of interest in their visitor-facing applications.

## Why they use Clearance

Organizations that use Clearance share a common need: **depending on OpenStreetMap data while controlling its quality**.

- They cannot afford erroneous edits affecting their services
- They don't have the resources to manually monitor all changes
- They want to contribute to OpenStreetMap while maintaining quality control
